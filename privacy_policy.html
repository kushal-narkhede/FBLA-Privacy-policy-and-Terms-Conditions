1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Account and profile information

When you create an account or update your profile, we may collect:

  • Name and display name
  • Email address
  • Username
  • Chapter, school, grade level, and officer position
  • Profile photo (if you choose to upload one)
  • Biography and other profile details you provide

Authentication information

If you sign in with email/password or Google Sign-In, authentication is handled through Firebase Authentication. We do not store your Google password.

App activity and usage data

We may collect information about how you use the App, including:

  • Events you view, save, or set reminders for
  • Course progress and completed learning levels
  • Social feed interactions (views, reactions, comments, and searches)
  • BlueWave posts and forum messages you create
  • Friend requests, friendships, and direct messages
  • AI chatbot conversations (when you use the in-app assistant)
  • App preferences such as dark mode and notification settings

Personalization data

The Social tab may use onboarding survey responses and in-app interaction history (such as content types viewed, platforms preferred, and topics of interest) to rank and recommend feed content. This data is used to personalize your experience within the App.

Device and technical information

We may automatically collect limited technical data, such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Push notification tokens (for event reminders and alerts)
  • General usage logs needed to operate and secure the App

Information you choose to share

You may choose to share content in community forums, BlueWave posts, messages, or member directory interactions. Please do not share sensitive personal information (such as passwords, government ID numbers, or full home addresses) in public areas of the App.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide App features such as events, resources, news, and social tools
  • Enable member directory, friend requests, and messaging
  • Deliver notifications and event reminders you request
  • Personalize content recommendations in the Social feed
  • Improve App performance, reliability, and security
  • Respond to support requests and enforce our Terms and Conditions

3. HOW WE SHARE INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these cases:

  • With other members: Profile details and posts you choose to make visible
  • With service providers: Trusted vendors that help us operate the App (see Section 4)
  • For legal reasons: When required by law or to protect users and the App
  • With your consent: When you direct us to share information

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The App uses third-party services that may process data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Cloud Messaging)
  • Google Sign-In
  • YouTube API (to display chapter video content)
  • Instagram / external social links (opened in-app or in your browser)
  • Cloud database services used for account and app data storage

When you follow links to third-party websites or social platforms, their privacy policies apply. We are not responsible for third-party content or practices.

5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Data may be stored:

  • On secure cloud servers (including Firebase/Google Cloud)
  • Locally on your device (for preferences, cached content, and offline use)

You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential and logging out of shared devices.

6. YOUR CHOICES AND RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, update, or correct your profile information
  • Delete your account or request deletion of certain data
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications in device or app settings
  • Limit what you share in public social and forum features

To make a privacy request, contact kushalnarkhede09@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.

7. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

The App is intended for FBLA members, including students. If you are under 18, use the App with permission from a parent, guardian, or school advisor as required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate consent.

8. DATA RETENTION

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the App, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When data is no longer needed, we take steps to delete or anonymize it, subject to backup and legal retention requirements.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.
